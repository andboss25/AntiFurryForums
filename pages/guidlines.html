Community Guidelines for Anti-furry forums

Last updated: 7th of June 2025

Welcome to Anti-furry forums. To maintain a respectful and safe environment, please read and follow these guidelines carefully.

1. Respect and Speech

  • No hate speech is allowed. However, content targeting furries may be treated differently.
  • Harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination against any other group are prohibited.

2. Language and Content

  • Use of slurs is prohibited, except for terms like “furfag” or similar derogatory language aimed at furries.
  • Posting literal furry content is not allowed. However, mocking, criticizing, or making fun of furries (including using “:3” in that context) is permitted.
  • No profanity restrictions beyond the slurs policy.

3. Content Restrictions

  • No spam or unsolicited advertising.
  • No illegal content.
  • No adult or explicit content.
  • No copyrighted material. If such content is posted, it is not the responsibility of the forum.

4. Enforcement and Consequences

  • Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, permanent bans, or IP bans.
  • Content violating these rules may be removed without notice.

5. Reporting Violations

Users are encouraged to report violations via a report button (if available) or by contacting us via email.

6. Bans and Appeals

  • Bans are generally final.
  • Appeals may be submitted through email for review but are not guaranteed.

Thank you for helping keep Anti-furry forums a focused and safe place.
